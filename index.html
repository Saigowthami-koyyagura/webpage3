Road Accessability

The Pulicat Lake Bird Sanctuary is 10 kilometers by road from Sullurpet Railway Station.
Road Accessibility – To reach Pulicat the National Highway
5 from Chennai has to be taken towards Nellore, and further on to
the Thatchoor Kootu Road to Ponneri village, which is roughly
19 km away from Pulicat village.
